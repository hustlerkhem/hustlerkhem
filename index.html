Who am i

Education shouldn't feel like a chore. That's the philosophy that drives me as a passionate teacher. Since starting my teaching career in 2023, I've embraced innovative methods to make learning engaging and fun. My Journey: Currently pursuing my MBA at Lincoln University College, I'm constantly seeking ways to bridge the gap between theory and practical application in the classroom. This journey began with a desire to go beyond just delivering lectures. I wanted to ignite a spark in students, to empower them to discover their own unique potential. Unlocking Potential Through Play: I've found that gamifying lessons fosters a positive and interactive learning environment. By turning complex concepts into engaging activities, I witness students gaining confidence and a deeper understanding of the material. A Lifelong Learner: Fueled by an insatiable curiosity, I believe there's something unique to learn from everything around us. This constant exploration allows me to bring fresh perspectives and innovative approaches to the classroom. Building a Positive Mindset: A positive learning environment is key to fostering growth. I strive to create a supportive and encouraging space where students feel comfortable taking risks and asking questions. Let's Learn Together! I'm always eager to connect with fellow educators, students, and anyone passionate about making education a transformative experience. Feel free to reach out and share your thoughts on how we can unlock potential through playful learning!
